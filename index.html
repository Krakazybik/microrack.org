About

MICRORACK is the most accessible modular synthesizer placed right on the breadboard. It is offering an impressive sound variability while being affordable and easy to learn.

MICRORACK is super portable. Its compact design, DC and USB-C power compatibility mean you can easily pack it in a bag and take it anywhere. Use a power bank to enjoy modular synthesis on the go - it's like having a portable rave!.

We want to make modular synthesis accessible and not expensive, while promoting education in sound and electronics. Our goal is to lower the learning curve of synthesis for newcomers by providing tutorials and step-by-step guides.

To further expand MICRORACK within the community, we plan to open-source our platform and modules shortly after our crowdfunding campaign.
